10-minute cheat's fried rice!

Quick dinner? Make the most of store-bought ingredients like frozen peas and packet rice and you'll have this Asian-inspired dish on the table in 10 minutes..

Who wants to go home and have to make food? certainly not me. Which is why i am bringing you this dish, it is delicious, can be made with ingredients available at home, and most importantly, it is very easy to make. With a recipe that has been passed down for generations, this meal is guranteed to leave you satisfied.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 tbsp peanut oil
  • 250g pork mince
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 long fresh red chilli
  • 1 1/2 tbsp kecap manis
  • 450g pkt microwave jasmine rice
  • 110g (2/3 cup) frozen peas
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sweet chilli sauce
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • Fried shallots, to serve

STEPS

  1. Heat the oil in a wok over high heat. Add the pork and cook, stirring often, for 3 minutes or until browned.
  2. While the pork is cooking, chop the garlic and chilli.
  3. Push the pork to 1 side of the wok and add the garlic to the empty side. Cook garlic, stirring, for 30 seconds then toss into the pork. Add 1/2 tablespoon kecap manis and stir to combine. Cook, stirring, for 30 seconds or until caramelised.
  4. Use your hands to break up the rice in the packet. Add the rice and peas to the wok. Cook, stirring, for 30 seconds. Push the mixture to 1 side of the wok and crack the eggs into the empty side. Cook the eggs for 15 seconds then chop in the wok and toss through the rice.
  5. Add the soy sauce, sweet chilli sauce and remaining 1tbs kecap manis to the wok. Toss to coat. Serve sprinkled with the basil, chilli and fried shallots.
